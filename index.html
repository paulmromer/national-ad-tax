Taxing Digital Advertising

Paul Romer

May 17, 2021

If the citizens of the United States want to limit the harm from a business model for digital services that relies on spying and manipulation, they can tell their legislators to impose a progressive tax on revenue from digital advertizing. I described elsewhere the general reasons for having confidence in this approach. Here, I delve into specifics.

The US Revenue Base

The key elements in a progressive tax are the tax base and the schedule of tax rates. One simple way to design a tax is to use as the base the revenue that a firm collects for displaying ads in the United States. This table illustrates a possible set of brackets and rates that could be applied to this base.

With this scedule, a firm that recieves less than $5 billion in revenue per year would pay no tax. A firm that collects $8 billion per year would pay a 5% rate on the $3 billion in revenue that falls into the second bracket, hence $15 million. Its average tax rate is this amount divided by its revenue of $8 billion -- 0.19%.

The line in this graph shows what you get if you repeat this calculation of the average tax rate for annual ad revenue between 0 and 60 billion. Using published estimates of US digital advertizing revenue for 2021, the graph uses dots and labels to show revenue and average tax rate for the four firms that would pay any tax under this particular schedule. The amount below the label for each firm shows how much it would owe (in billions of dollars).

The next table shows that total revenue from digital ads has been growing very rapidly and is projected to continue to be rapid. If this tax schedule had been in place since 2018 and if revenue were unchanged, the third column shows that total tax reciepts for the government would grow even more rapidly.

These estimates of additional funds for the government are large. Over 10 years, they would finance a substantial portion of the $2 trillion in spending proposed under the administration's infrastructure plan. But if this tax were enacted, the funds collected by the government would soon start to fall. If they do, it will be a sign that this the tax is working as expected.
